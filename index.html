Getting Started

How can I get started?

An easy project for one person might be difficult for someone else. Therefore we have broken down the projects as to either requiring no programming experience and rely on plug and play systems. You will notice that with certain eeg headsets that there is more tutorials than others. This is likely due to the ease of development with certain eeg form factors.

The best way to get started will depend on the headset that you have available.

  • Muse
    • Seeing Your Data
    • Intro to BCI
    • Building a Neurogame
  • OpenBCI
  • Emotiv
  • NeuroSky
  • No HeadSet
